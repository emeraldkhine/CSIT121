About Me

Hello, I'm Emerald.

I am an Information Technology and Business student at CCBC. I am interested in business analysis and data, especially how companies use information to make decisions and solve problems. That interest naturally led me to learning more about the technical side of systems and databases and how they are used in real businesses.
