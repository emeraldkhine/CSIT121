About This Image

This is a simple image of overlapping circles with bright colors orange, cyan, and purple on a dark blue background. I made it myself using Nano Banana for this assignment.

Why I Chose This Image

I picked this kind of image because PNG is the best choice when you have sharp edges and flat, bold colors. The circles in this image have very clean outlines. If I saved this as a JPG instead, those edges would look blurry and messy.

PNG also supports transparent backgrounds, which is really useful for logos and icons. You can put them on any color background and they look clean. That is something JPG simply cannot do.
